What is a Multi-Disciplinary Panel (Tumor Board) Review?

Tumor board review is a multidisciplinary team effort to extensively review and evaluate the medical diagnosis and treatment options available to a particular patient. Such committees will most likely be comprised of surgical, medical and radiation oncologists, along with radiology therapists and pathologists.

Why should you go for Tumor Board Review?

Since cancer treatments can be majorly categorized into surgery, chemotherapy and radiation, and each of these have their own specialty oncologists, it is best to consult with the three oncologists together. Cancer treatment is mostly irreversible, and a mis-diagnosis can be cost a life. Hence, we help you with

Getting a detailed report from three expert oncologists with different specialization

Removing biases from treatment plan through combined opinion

Fee of Tumor Board Review:

The fee of ZenOnco.io Tumour Board Review is Rs 4,000 to Rs 7,000, depending on individual consultation fee of each panel oncologist.
